About Us

Malimali Ventures (U) Ltd is a Ugandan based business establised in 2016 with an aim of promoting, selling and bringing affordable but yet fresh fruits and commodities to the people of Uganda and the East African community at large. the business first started with only one trading and selling item at the beginning which where managoes and these where largely sold and bought by wholesellers and retailers. One year later in 2017, the business added other items to its carts which where Apples and graps which are largely imported from countries such as China, South Africa, Spain, Italy and India.For the past three years in operations, the business has grown from having only one operating store but now with two stores in Operations, with the latest located in the city centre Kampala
